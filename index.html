Crystals Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the statrt of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystals, you will add a specific amount of points to your total scores.

You win the game by matching your total score to target number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the target number.

The value of each crystals is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystals.
